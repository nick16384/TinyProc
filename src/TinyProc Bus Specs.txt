CPU internal bus:
Width: 48 bits
Structure:
8 bits: Addressed subcomponent
9th bit: Read (0); Write (1)
10th - 16th bit: Reserved for later use
32 bits: Data

Subcomponents:
0x00 - CU PC
0x01 - CU IRA
0x02 - CU IRB
0x10 - MMU MAR
0x11 - MMU MDR
0x20 - ALU X
0x21 - ALU Y
0x22 - ALU Config (Rightmost bits)
0x23 - ALU Result (Acc)
0xFF - <highest possible value>
